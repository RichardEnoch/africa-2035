About the Event

A Pan-African Movement for the
Next Generation of QS Leaders

AFRICA 2035 is a high-impact leadership roundtable organised by IQSSF — bringing together the sharpest Quantity Surveying student minds from across Africa to share ideas, challenge the status quo, and collectively shape a sustainable, innovative future for the profession.

Organised by IQSSF

The International Quantity Surveying Students Federation — the definitive pan-African authority for QS student leadership.

Interactive Roundtable Format

Your voice shapes the conversation. Submit questions ahead of time, respond to live polls, and engage panelists directly.

Actionable Leadership Insights

Walk away with real strategies to become a forward-thinking, innovative QS professional ready to lead Africa's future.

Why Attend

The Event You Simply Cannot Miss

Don't just watch Africa's QS future unfold — be part of the conversation that shapes it.

Build Continental Connections

Network with QS student leaders across Nigeria, Ghana, Uganda, Kenya, and Tanzania. Forge bonds that transcend borders.

Gain Expert Insights

Panelists share actionable strategies on innovation and sustainability in QS — straight from Africa's most inspiring student leaders.

Represent Your Country

Carry your nation's voice into a continental conversation. Your ideas and questions will influence the future of QS across Africa.

Drive Innovation in QS

Explore how technology, PropTech, and forward-thinking practice are reshaping the profession — and how you can lead that change.

Champion Sustainability

Discover how QS students are driving green construction, responsible procurement, and sustainable urban development across Africa.

100% Virtual & Free

Join live from anywhere on X Space. No tickets, no travel, no barriers — just ideas, leadership, and the future of African QS.
